About the Game

UnderAttack is a 2D-platformer about the human immune system, inspired by the COVID-19 Pandemic. The game aims to be a source of entertainment in the midst of the #StayHome movement and provides interesting factoids about the human system and pathogens.

The concept of the game was born in a hostel Lounge, in which both of the Creators were bored out of their minds and had nothing to do for a 3-month-long summer vacation (a vacation where they unfortunately can't go home). This game was developed during said summer vacation for a university project.
